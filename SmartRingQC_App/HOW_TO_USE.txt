SMART RING QC INSPECTOR — HOW TO USE
=====================================

1. Connect microscope to laptop via USB
2. Double-click SmartRingQC.exe to open
3. Press SPACEBAR to capture a ring photo
4. Review the image on screen
5. Press A = Accept, R = Reject, W = Rework
6. Press Z = Undo last action
7. Press S = Skip current image

All images are saved automatically.
Reports are saved in the reports/ folder.
Each day gets its own folder automatically.

KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS:
SPACE = Capture photo
A     = Accept
R     = Reject
W     = Rework
S     = Skip
Z     = Undo
